## Preview Distribution Scope
- **Store Footprint:** Limited to agreed flagship and concept stores with controlled traffic patterns.
- **User Eligibility:** Opt-in associates and store managers; no consumer-facing interactions during pilot.
- **Device Coverage:** Smart glasses assigned to designated staff with unique IDs for auditability.
- **Feature Set:** Inventory lookup, guided picking, planogram verification, and task confirmation flows.
- **Content Limitations:** No personalized promotions or customer PII capture during preview.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Task Efficiency:** Time to pick/stock items vs. baseline SOP.
- **Accuracy:** Reduction in inventory discrepancies and mis-picks.
- **Adoption:** Daily active devices and session completion rates per store.
- **Reliability:** Crash-free sessions and successful syncs per shift.
- **Feedback Quality:** Number of actionable UX/ops improvements logged per week.

## Operating Cadence
- **Standups:** Weekly joint review of incidents, feature readiness, and store rollout gating.
- **Ops Reporting:** Store-level dashboard shared twice per week with SLA exceptions highlighted.
- **Change Management:** Feature toggles coordinated 48 hours before enablement; rollback plan documented.
- **Onsite Support:** Shadowing during first 3 business days of activation at each location.

## SDK Mock Requirements
- **Data Sources:** Mock endpoints for catalog, inventory status, and task assignments with stable schemas.
- **Latency Targets:** Mocks should respond within 300ms p95 to simulate production-grade performance.
- **Offline Mode:** Provide canned responses for intermittent connectivity; cache invalidation intervals noted.
- **Event Logging:** Simulated telemetry for task start/complete, device health, and error codes for analytics validation.

## Privacy & Safety Commitments
- **Data Minimization:** No collection of customer images, payment data, or audio; associate identifiers are pseudonymous.
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access for store managers vs. associates; audit logs retained for the pilot duration.
- **Safe Operation:** Hands-free workflows tested for line-of-sight safety; emergency stop/voice commands enabled.
- **Incident Handling:** Joint escalation matrix with 24/7 contact for critical safety or privacy events.
